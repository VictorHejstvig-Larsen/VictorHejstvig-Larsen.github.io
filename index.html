Welcome to mu page describing my PhD Project with the title "How Politicians Learn to Predict Voter Behaviour"

I am currently pursuing my Ph.D. in Political Science at Aarhus University. My research area is centered in representation, where I'm focusing on politicians perceptions of voter attitudes. My project focuses specifically on how politicians learn to predict voter reactions to policy.

On this website, you'll find information about my academic journey, research projects, publications, and datasets related to my work.
